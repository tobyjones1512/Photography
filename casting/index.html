What is boudoir?

Boudoir is a genre of portraiture centered on intimacy, self-image, and personal expression - not exhibitionism. The goal is empowerment, self-love, and confidence. It is a deeply personal and transformative experience for most subjects.

Subjects are typically photographed in lingerie, bra and panties, or topless. Every session is guided by artistry and authenticity - you will never be asked to do anything outside your comfort zone.
